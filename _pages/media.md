## Overview

My work contributes to public debate on public sector reform, workforce policy, social democracy, and multicultural representation.

This includes opinion pieces, commentary, and media engagement across national and international outlets.

---

## Featured Commentary

**I was born in Australia. Why do I need to renounce the Chinese Communist Party? (Sydney Morning Herald, 2020)**  
Reflects on the experience of being asked to “condemn” the Chinese Communist Party at a parliamentary inquiry, arguing that such loyalty tests are harmful to democratic discourse and social cohesion. 【1-529396】  

**Australia’s Public Sector Is Far Smaller Than Debates Suggest (Per Capita, 2025)**  
Challenges prevailing narratives about public sector size and highlights the implications for workforce capability and service delivery.

**Australians Are Craving Better Work-Life Balance (Canberra Times, 2025)**  
Outlines practical policy solutions to improve work-life balance, including institutional reforms.

**A Working Definition of Sinophobia Would Improve the China Debate (Lowy Institute, 2024)**  
Proposes clearer conceptual frameworks to improve public discourse and policy debate.
